About the Cluster

TheClusterFlux is a personal Kubernetes cluster that serves as both a learning platform and hosting environment for various projects. The cluster is designed to be a playground for experimentation with cloud-native technologies while also providing reliable hosting for applications and services.

This platform allows creators to deploy their projects in a containerized environment, making it easy to test, deploy, and share their work. Whether you're a developer looking to host your latest creation or someone interested in learning about Kubernetes, TheClusterFlux provides the infrastructure you need.
